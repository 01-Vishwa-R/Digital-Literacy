Stay Safe Online – Simple Tips

- Don’t click on links from unknown or suspicious messages
- Always take a moment to check who sent the email
- Never share your passwords or OTPs with anyone
- Use strong passwords and avoid using the same one everywhere
- Turn on two-factor authentication for extra security
- Double-check internship offers or job messages on official websites
- Avoid making payments or logging in on public Wi-Fi
- Be careful with messages that sound urgent or threatening

For money safety:
- Never share your UPI PIN with anyone
- Always confirm payment requests before accepting them

If something goes wrong, report it at cybercrime.gov.in  
Helpline: 1930
